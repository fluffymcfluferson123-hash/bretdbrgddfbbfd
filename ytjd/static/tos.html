PHSP privacy policy and terms of use

Section 1: Definitions

In this privacy policy, "We" refers to "PHSP". "Personally-identifiable information" (PII) refers to information that can be used to identify a person such as IP addresses. "Official PHSP deployments" refer to the PHSP application running on the official server. "PHSP deployment operator" refers to the person(s) running the PHSP application. "ToS" refers to Terms of Service.

Please redirect any general inquiries to our official community on Discord. Redirect any questions regarding unofficial PHSP deployments to its operator.

Section 2: Summary

  • By using PHSP, you agree you understand and acknowledge this privacy policy and its clauses. This page has been created to help you understand what types of data we collect, how we use and keep your data safe, etc. If you do not agree with this privacy policy and terms of service, you must cease use of our services.
  • PHSP will not be held liable in any situation that may occur as a result of using or hosting the PHSP application on ANY PHSP deployment.
  • The safest way to use PHSP is by using its official deployments or by hosting PHSP on your own machine.

Section 2.1: Summary - Official PHSP Deployment

  • Official PHSP deployments are run with privacy in mind. We do temporarily collect and retain some PII such as IP addresses used to access the official PHSP deployment, but this is not intentional.

Section 2.2: Summary - Unofficial PHSP Deployments

  • PHSP developers are committed to ensuring PHSP remains a secure application. However, we cannot guarantee the same level of security or privacy of unofficial PHSP deployments.
  • The type of information collected on community deployments is completely dependent on who and where the deployment is being hosted.
  • Generally, we recommend PHSP deployment operators collect as minimal information as possible.
  • We cannot control unofficial PHSP deployments.
  • We cannot verify the authenticity of the code in unofficial PHSP deployments. This is an extremely rare situation, but we will not be held responsible if your data such as an account becomes compromised as a result of malicious code in an unofficial PHSP deployment.

Section 5: The information we collect

Section 5.1: The official PHSP deployment

  • Information that may be unintentionally collected as part of using PHSP's official deployment may include basic information such as your IP address, web traffic when using PHSP, etc. This information is temporary and is kept until the server is rebooted.
  • We do not purposefully collect anyone's information. We try to limit the data collected by the official PHSP deployment.
  • We do not, will not, and will never knowingly collect the information of minors under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by laws in your country).

Section 5.2: Unofficial PHSP Deployments

  • We can't give a definitive answer on what information is collected when you use an unofficial PHSP deployment; only the operator of the PHSP deployment you're using can.
  • We urge operators to collect and store as minimal information as possible, but we can't control what they do.

Section 6: Control

  • We do not have the power to control what unofficial PHSP deployment operators do with their deployment.
  • We protect information stored on the official PHSP deployment by limiting access. The only person that has access to the server on which the official PHSP deployment runs is the server host and the owner of PHSP, xbubbo.
  • We cannot check every deployment for malicious code. If any data such as your account gets compromised after you access it on an unofficial PHSP deployment, we will not be held liable.

Section 7: How we use basic information on the official PHSP deployment that may be collected accidentally

  • Your IP address is used to allow you to use the PHSP service. This is how it works for every website you visit, not just PHSP. The difference is whether or not this data is intentionally collected and stored.
  • Info about your web traffic while using PHSP is needed to allow you to access the service you wish to access.
  • PHSP will NEVER sell your info to third parties, other than to some ad companies. We do this so we can make enough money to pay for our server.
  • The official PHSP server operator may respond to legal requests and assist law enforcement officials in legal investigations if ever needed. We can't guarantee we'll always have information to hand out as the information that may be collected is automatically destroyed on reboot, but if we do still have information relevant to the investigation, we may provide law enforcement officials with it. Please redirect all requests regarding data with an unofficial PHSP deployment to its operator, as we don't have control over unofficial deployments and couldn't comply with requests even if we wanted to.
